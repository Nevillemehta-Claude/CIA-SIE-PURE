## Design Decisions

### Color Palette: Bright & Motivational
- **Primary Backgrounds:** Pure White (#FFFFFF), Snow (#FAFBFC), Cloud (#F4F5F7)
- **Accent Colors:** Teal (#0D9488), Coral (#F97316), Violet (#8B5CF6)
- **Semantic Colors:** Emerald for BULLISH, Rose for BEARISH, Slate for NEUTRAL
- **Freshness Colors:** Green (CURRENT), Amber (RECENT), Red (STALE)

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans - Modern, professional, excellent readability
- **Monospace Font:** JetBrains Mono - For prices, codes, timestamps, data

### Constitutional Compliance Built-In
- **CR-002 (Equal Visual Weight):** All contradiction cards have identical sizing
- **CR-003 (Mandatory Disclaimer):** Cannot be dismissed, always visible
- **Prohibited Elements:** No confidence scores, weights, recommendations, action buttons
