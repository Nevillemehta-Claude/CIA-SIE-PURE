## PURPOSE

This module contains universal standards for project governance - how decisions are made, how changes are controlled, how quality is ensured, and how work is organized. These principles apply regardless of project type, team size, or methodology.

---

## SCOPE

### What This Module Covers

- Decision-making frameworks
- Change control processes
- Quality gates and stage gates
- Risk management principles
- Stakeholder management
- Requirements governance
- Configuration management
- Audit and compliance principles

### What This Module Does NOT Cover

- Project-specific governance rules (see PROJECT_CHRONICLES)
- Technical implementation details (see DEVELOPMENT module)
- Specific tool configurations (reference tool docs)

## KEY PRINCIPLE

> "Governance is not bureaucracy. Governance is the framework that enables consistent, traceable, reversible decision-making."

Good governance accelerates delivery by preventing rework, reducing ambiguity, and ensuring alignment.
